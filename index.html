Extracurricular Ecosystem

The Instech Students Sports and Cultural Council (ISSACC) manages the vibrant extracurricular ecosystem at IET Lucknow. We cater to over 2,800 students, deeply rooted in the motto "Gyanam Bharah Kriyam Bina".

Holistic Development

Nurturing Talent &
Community Spirit.

Balancing the rigors of an engineering curriculum, ISSACC acts as an incubator for non-technical skills. We provide practical platforms for students to learn logistics, sponsorship, marketing, and conflict resolution.
